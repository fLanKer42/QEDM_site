Internships



The Branch has a unique 6 month internship in 8th semester. 8th semester internship can be used for Foreign Training or an internship in the field of interest. This branch is also the only one to sit for two CDC internships, all other branches only have one in their pre-final year.

Companies where QEDM students did internships: PhonePe, Capillary, NUS, Mercedes-Benz, EY, Grofers, Inria France.


Placements



The branch had only 9 seven students graduating in 2020, hence the sample size for placements is very small. Some students got Pre Placement Offers from their 6 month internships.
QEDM students are allowed to sit for any company that opens for industrial department, this includes a majority of the companies coming to KGP.

Companies that have hired QEDM students in the past include: Bidgeley, ZS, PWC, Standard Chartered, Robert Bosch, Barclays, EXL, Sprinklr and Adrosonic.

Overview of QEDM


  • 5 year dual degree program that offers B.Tech in Engineering Design and Manufacturing and M.Tech in Design and Quality Engineering. Started in 2013.
  • Offers 2 verticals: Mechanical and Industrial Electronics. Students have to choose the vertical at the end of first year.
  • Courses from Industrial and Systems Engineering, Electrical, Electronics and Mechanical departments are covered in the course, giving students enough opportunity to explore interests in multiple fields
  • A 6 month internship during the 8th semester is a unique feature, QEDM is the only branch that has the opportunity to sit for CDC internship twice during third and fourth year.By the end of the course, a QEDM student has a minimum of 8 month work experience.
  • Due to being a relatively new and small branch, QEDM students face difficulty of creating awareness about the branch. Companies that come for CDC, look for 2 month pre-final year interns. But the off-campus opportunities for the students are plenty.
  • Placement record of QEDM has been 100% for the past two years with students being placed in companies like Bidgeley, ZS, PWC, Standard Chartered, Robert Bosch, Barclays, EXL, Sprinklr and Adrosonic. Every company that opens for Industrial department during placements, opens for QEDM as well.
  • The inter-disciplinery specialisations of Financial Engineering and Entrepreneurship Engineering aren't applicable for the branch. But department change is possible in accordance with the institute policy of dual degree courses.

Academic Avenues



Industrial Electronics curriculum is parallel with most of Electrical Engineering Branch. Until the 7th semester, most courses are common with electrical.So, you can have research opportunities in fields such as Power Electronics, Electrical Machines, Embedded Systems.

Mechanical Engineering vertical has almost all core subjects and laboratories of mechanical engineering. The possible research areas are Quality Engineering, Geometric modeling and other mechanical core subjects. Though the courses only provide introductory knowledge and solid knowledge base is required by an individual in their own capacity.

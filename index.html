Quality Engineering Design and Manufacturing (QEDM)

About

This is a unique program concieved at IIT Kharagpur to inculcate in students an enhanced awareness of Engineering Design, Manufacturing and Quality issues related either to Mechanical Engineering Products such as Automotive systems or to Electronic Products such as Real-time embedded Systems. The academic contents of the program are accordingly designed to expose the students to major engineering aspects related to products including Product Modelling and development, Design for quality, Manufacturability and Assembly.

The 5-year dual degree curriculum in Quality Engineering Design and Manufacturing (QEDM) is proposed in two verticals - Mechanical Engineering and Industrial Electronics. For each of the vertical, there will be B. Tech (Hons) in Engineering Product Design and Manufacturing and M. Tech in Design and Quality Engineering.
